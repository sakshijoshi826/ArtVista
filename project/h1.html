Every artist, beginner or professional, will need a little pick-me-up once in a while, to get over a bad piece of work they thought would turn out well or to overcome artist’s block. Here are my favourite inspirational art quotes, from painters to philosophers, from scientists to photographers.

Inspiration is for amateurs. The rest of us just show up and get the work done. If you wait around for the clouds to part and a bolt of lightning to strike you in the brain, you're not going to make an awful lot of work. ― Chuck Close

Art is a line around your thoughts. ― Gustav Klimt
